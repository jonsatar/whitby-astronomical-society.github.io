We aim to bring a more thorough understanding and appreciation for the Cosmos. We learn and appreciate best when we have a sense of community. Although from a different time and field, John D. Community

A sense of community is critical for the development of any human being let alone any person that is interested in deverloping a skill set. Begin your journey with us and get a few pointers towards a head start and begin either a possible career or simply just remain a hobbyist.

With various programs set to high school and elementary school outreach programs, gain some experience teaching and engaging with others in an authoritative position. We'll show you all the steps if you might be nervous and can prepare you with all sorts of materials to make your presentation and engagement a success.
