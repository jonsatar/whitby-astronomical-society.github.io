Introduction

What is the essential story being told by your site and what type of structure did you choose to implement.

The website is a community based organization that seeks to connect those interested in Space as well as related sciences. Since expertise in these fields is few and far between, a modern and attractive website would garner new and significant interest especially from youth who are most impacted by an early appreciation of such a difficult topic.

The website highlights several accomplishments that have occurred in the recent and far past through various banners, sliders, and news bulletins. It also features a traditional newsletter and interactive quiz for further engagement. Repeat visists are encouraged from contact and membership pages. An accordion was implemented to make more interactive those who may have questions about the role of the club.

Accessibility

State 3 ways in which your site is accessible.

  1. A dark background was incorporated throughout all the web pages of the website, with white text in order to reduce glare for those who are visibly impaired.

  2. A button toggle was incorporated to shut off the video in the banner in order to reduce motion that may affect some users who find such distracting elements difficult to read the main content.

  3. Alt tags were described throughout all the images especially for those who may be severely visually impaired.

Usability

State 3 ways in which you have considered usability in your site.

  1. A sticky top navigation bar that reaches all of the 5 webpages was incorporated into each. This would allow users to redirect if they become disinterested with a current section very easily.

  2. The navigation also includes drop down menus to allow for further navigability within the web pages themselves.

  3. Users are frequently redirected to the membership and registration page to encourage them to sign up and read more on some of the interesting content.
